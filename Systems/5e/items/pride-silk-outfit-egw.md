# Pride Silk Outfit
*Adventuring Gear*  

- **Cost**: 500 gp
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

An outfit made of pride silk weighs 4 pounds and costs 500 gp. If you aren't wearing armor, your base Armor Class is 11 + your Dexterity modifier while wearing it.
