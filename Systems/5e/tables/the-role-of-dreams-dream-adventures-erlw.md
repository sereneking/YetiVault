# The Role of Dreams; Dream Adventures

**Dream Adventures**

| dice: d4 | Adventure Goal |
|----------|----------------|
| 1 | Break a psychic lock that is preventing a House Thuranni spy from remembering what they learned about a sinister Dreaming Dark plot. |
