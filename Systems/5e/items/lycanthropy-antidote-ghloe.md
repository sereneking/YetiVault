# Lycanthropy Antidote
*Potion, rare*  


This silvery potion, made with [fzeg](/Systems/5e/bestiary/monstrosity/fzeg-ghloe.md) blood, is dotted with flecks of red. Drinking this liquid removes the curse of werewolf lycanthropy.
