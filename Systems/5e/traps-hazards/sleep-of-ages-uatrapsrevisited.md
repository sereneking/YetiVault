# Sleep of Ages
*Generic Hazard*  

A pressure plate unleashes a spell that threatens to send intruders into a deep slumber. The dungeon's guardians can then dispose of the sleepers.

*Source: Unearthed Arcana: Traps Revisited p. 3*
