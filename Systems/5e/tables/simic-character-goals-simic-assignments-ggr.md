**Simic Assignments**

`dice: [](simic-character-goals-simic-assignments-ggr.md#^simic-assignments)`

| dice: d6 | Assignment |
|----------|------------|
| 1 | Protect a Simic researcher who is trying to make an exhaustive examination of a new form of plant life. |
