***Martial Advantage.*** Once per turn, Grunka can deal an extra 7 (`2d6`) damage to a creature it hits with a weapon attack if that creature is within 5 feet of an ally of Grunka that isn't [incapacitated](/Systems/5e/rules/conditions.md#incapacitated).
