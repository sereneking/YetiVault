# Helm of Awareness
*Source: Tasha's Cauldron of Everything p. 21*  

**Prerequisite**: 10th-level

While wearing this helmet, a creature has advantage on initiative rolls. In addition, the wearer can't be [surprised](/Systems/5e/rules/conditions.md#surprised), provided it isn't [incapacitated](/Systems/5e/rules/conditions.md#incapacitated).
