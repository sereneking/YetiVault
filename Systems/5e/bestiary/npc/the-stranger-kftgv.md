## Traits

***Special Equipment.*** The Stranger wears an invisible [ring of mind shielding](/Systems/5e/items/ring-of-mind-shielding.md) that thwarts magical attempts to read their thoughts. The Stranger also wears [dimensional shackles](/Systems/5e/items/dimensional-shackles.md) but is eager to be rid of them. One touch from Omid's mace-key causes the shackles to fall off, freeing the Stranger.

***Magic Resistance.*** The Stranger has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.

***Spellcasting.*** The Stranger is an 18th-level spellcaster. Its spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 17, `+9` to hit with spell attacks). The Stranger can cast [disguise self](/Systems/5e/spells/disguise-self.md) and [invisibility](/Systems/5e/spells/invisibility.md) at will and has the following wizard spells prepared:
