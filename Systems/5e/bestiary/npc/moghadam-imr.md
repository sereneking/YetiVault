## Traits

***Special Equipment.*** Moghadam wields [Ruinblade](/Systems/5e/items/ruinblade-imr.md) (see appendix C).

***Artificial Mind.*** Moghadam can manifest an artificial mind as a floating spectral image of a [demilich](/Systems/5e/bestiary/undead/demilich.md)'s jeweled skull. Moghadam can communicate telepathically with this mind, send it up to 300 feet away from him, and see and hear through it.

***Infoportation (1/Day).*** Moghadam can teleport himself into an unoccupied space next to his artificial mind.

***Spellcasting.*** Moghadam is an 18th-level spellcaster. His spellcasting ability is Intelligence (spell save DC 16, `+8` to hit with spell attacks). He has the following artificer spells prepared:

**Cantrips (at will)**: [dancing lights](/Systems/5e/spells/dancing-lights.md), [light](/Systems/5e/spells/light.md), [message](/Systems/5e/spells/message.md), [shocking grasp](/Systems/5e/spells/shocking-grasp.md)

**1st level (4 slots)**: [comprehend languages](/Systems/5e/spells/comprehend-languages.md), [cure wounds](/Systems/5e/spells/cure-wounds.md), [grease](/Systems/5e/spells/grease.md), [identify](/Systems/5e/spells/identify.md)

**2nd level (3 slots)**: [detect thoughts](/Systems/5e/spells/detect-thoughts.md), [invisibility](/Systems/5e/spells/invisibility.md), [heat metal](/Systems/5e/spells/heat-metal.md)

**3rd level (3 slots)**: [dispel magic](/Systems/5e/spells/dispel-magic.md), [flame arrows](/Systems/5e/spells/flame-arrows-xge.md), [hypnotic pattern](/Systems/5e/spells/hypnotic-pattern.md)

**4th level (3 slots)**: [fabricate](/Systems/5e/spells/fabricate.md), [stone shape](/Systems/5e/spells/stone-shape.md), [stoneskin](/Systems/5e/spells/stoneskin.md)

**5th level (1 slots)**: [creation](/Systems/5e/spells/creation.md)

## Actions

***Ruinblade.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `+6` to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. *Hit:* 9 (`2d6 + 2`) slashing damage.

***Blight (1/Day).*** While wielding [Ruinblade](/Systems/5e/items/ruinblade-imr.md), Moghadam can cast the [blight](/Systems/5e/spells/blight.md) spell (DC 15).

***Disintegrate (1/7 Days).*** While wielding [Ruinblade](/Systems/5e/items/ruinblade-imr.md), Moghadam can cast the [disintegrate](/Systems/5e/spells/disintegrate.md) spell against any nonmagical object or creation of magical force.

***Information Overload (Recharge 5-6).*** Moghadam overloads the thoughts of one creature within 5 feet of either himself or his artificial mind. The target must succeed on a DC 16 Intelligence saving throw or take 22 (`4d8 + 4`) psychic damage, and the next attack roll made against the target before the end of Moghadam's next turn has advantage.
