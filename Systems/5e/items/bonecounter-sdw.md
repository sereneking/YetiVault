# Bonecounter
*Melee Weapon, rare*  

- **Damage**: 1d6 B
- **Weight**: 4.0 lbs.

This weapon is a magical +2 mace called Bonecounter. Whenever this weapon is used to destroy an undead creature, a single silver piece appears in the wielder's pocket.

*Source: Sleeping Dragon's Wake*
