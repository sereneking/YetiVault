If the target is Medium or smaller, it is [grappled](/Systems/5e/rules/conditions.md#grappled) (escape DC 15) and must succeed on a DC 15 Intelligence saving throw or be [stunned](/Systems/5e/rules/conditions.md#stunned) until this grapple ends.

***Extract Brain.*** *Melee Weapon Attack:* `+7` to hit, reach 5 ft., one [incapacitated](/Systems/5e/rules/conditions.md#incapacitated) humanoid [grappled](/Systems/5e/rules/conditions.md#grappled) by Captain N'ghathrod. *Hit:* The target takes 55 (`10d10`) piercing damage. If this damage reduces the target to 0 hit points, Captain N'ghathrod kills the target by extracting and devouring its brain.

***Mind Blast (Recharge 5-6).*** Captain N'ghathrod magically emits psychic energy in a 60-foot cone. Each creature in that area must succeed on a DC 15 Intelligence saving throw or take 22 (`4d8 + 4`) psychic damage and be [stunned](/Systems/5e/rules/conditions.md#stunned) for 1 minute. A creature can repeat the saving throw at the end of each of its turns, ending the effect on itself on a success.
