# Grasp of Hadar
*Source: Unearthed Arcana: Revised Class Options p. 6*  

**Prerequisite**: [eldritch blast](/Systems/5e/spells/eldritch-blast.md) cantrip

Once on each of your turns when you hit a creature with your [eldritch blast](/Systems/5e/spells/eldritch-blast.md) cantrip, you can move that creature in a straight line 10 feet closer to yourself.
