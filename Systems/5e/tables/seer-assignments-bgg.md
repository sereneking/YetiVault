*Source: Bigby Presents: Glory of the Giants p. 60* 

**Seer Assignments**

`dice: [](seer-assignments-bgg.md#^seer-assignments)`

| dice: d6 | Assignment |
|----------|------------|
| 1 | Help the giant re-create an ancient ritual that should reveal an omen or revelation from Annam. |
