# Dragongleam
*Melee Weapon, unknown (magic)*  

- **Damage**:

  - Two-handed: 1d8 P
- **Range**: 20/60
- **Properties**: [Thrown](/Systems/5e/rules/item-properties.md#Thrown), [Versatile](/Systems/5e/rules/item-properties.md#Versatile)
