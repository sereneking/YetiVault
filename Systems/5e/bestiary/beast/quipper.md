# Quipper
*Source: Monster Manual p. 335, Princes of the Apocalypse, Tomb of Annihilation, Ghosts of Saltmarsh, Mythic Odysseys of Theros, Icewind Dale: Rime of the Frostmaiden, Critical Role: Call of the Netherdeep, Plane Shift: Amonkhet, Turn of Fortune's Wheel. Available in the SRD and the Basic Rules.*  

A quipper is a carnivorous fish with sharp teeth.

Quippers can adapt to any aquatic environment, including cold subterranean lakes. They frequently gather in swarms; the statistics for a swarm of quippers appear later in this appendix.
